Who We Are

A-Swift (Automating Africa, Swiftly) is a forward-thinking software consulting firm specialising in WhatsApp automation and decision support systems. We help businesses across Africa connect with their clients faster and smarter—right where they already are: on WhatsApp. At A-Swift, we build tailor-made solutions that streamline communication, automate workflows, and enhance customer experiences. From intelligent chatbots to dynamic decision tools, every system we design is custom-built to our client’s exact needs—and delivered at a fraction of the traditional cost. Driven by innovation and local insight, we’re on a mission to make powerful technology accessible, affordable, and transformational for businesses of all sizes.
